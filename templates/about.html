ABOUT

University Prep is a 6-12 independent school in Seattle that focuses on fostering intellectual courage and social responsibility. To celebrate the opening of the new ULab building, UPrep is proud to announce the first iteration of UPrep Model United Nations (UPrep MUN). With an emphasis on critical thinking skills and diplomacy, the conference strives to create a platform for debate, collaboration, and problem-solving in the context of real-world issues. UPrep MUN is led by the passionate students of UPrep’s MUN club. The UPrep MUN club believes the conference will build and empower the next generation of leaders.
